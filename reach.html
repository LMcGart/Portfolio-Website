Project Description

Over the course of this project I worked on a team of 5 consultants in collaboration with the School of Kinesiology at the University of Michigan. Our role was to make design suggestions for the current Michigan Model for Health and InPact websites. These websites house much of the physical education curriculum for the state of Michigan. Over the course of the project we had weekly team and client meetings in order to stay on track and meet deadlines. To back our suggestions, we interviewed teachers and health-coordinators from around the state to better understand what the current websites did, and didn't, do well. Our final deliverable consisted of a write-up describing our design suggestions and why we made them. We also included a figma mock-up of the design changes we were proposing the clients add to their InPact website. I was responsible for interviews and designing the figma mock-up. It was an amazing learning experience that taught me hands-on the UX design process from start to finish. For more information about this project, please contact me at lmcgart@umich.edu.
