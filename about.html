About Me

2002 - Present

Hi, I'm Liam!

I'm a junior at the University of Michigan majoring in Information with a focus in UX design. On campus, I'm involved in a tech consulting club, Reach Consulting Group, and The Michigan Daily, the university's campus newspaper. I do UX design and web development work for these organizations. In school, I'm most interested in how humans interact with technology, especially social media. Outside of school, I'm very passionate about climbing, cooking, hiking, photography, travel, and mechanical keyboards. I love great design, whether that be appealing aesthetics or exceptional usability/accessibility. As a result, I'm always searching for things in my everyday life to inspire my work!
